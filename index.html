About: The Waifutomy Project

Waifutomy was consolidated as a solo band project in 2018, releasing their first demo single "Erotic Sodomy" that same year. Since then, the project has continued to release music in the genre of Slam, Grind, Noise with anime and guro themes. Throughout the years, the project has had several materials in its discography, as well as collaborations with other bands and projects around the world. Waifutomy still continues to produce music within the genres in which it operates, continuing this personal project.
